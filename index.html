Rachel Hyman

I'm a Mobile Engineer at Superhuman and am based out of Chicago. I think it's the best city in the world. On the side I'm developing the iOS app for Italic Type, which is creating technology for avid readers. I have given technical talks about location-based apps, accessibility, and emoji and strings at conferences like NSScotland and 360iDev.

I sell my zines and artwork on Etsy. Stay tuned for a new zine project coming soon!

I started a literary journal and a publishing project based around Chicago neighborhoods. I also co-curated a local reading series called Welcome to the Neighborhood. I have performed my own work at the Museum of Contemporary Art Chicago and am the author of a poetry chapbook, Dear S (Big Lucks, 2015).

In my not-so-copious free time, I enjoy baking, reading, and hanging with my rescue shiba, Kenny.
